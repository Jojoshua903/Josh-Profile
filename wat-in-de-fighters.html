HOW TO PLAY

THE BASICS

ATTACK
Melee hit. Short range, low cooldown.
SPECIAL
Each fighter's signature move — see the movelist below.
UP-SPECIAL
A recovery move that also launches upward.
SUPER
A powerful move once your super meter is full. Meter charges as you deal and take damage.
SHIELD
Hold to block incoming hits. Your shield can break if it takes too much punishment.
JUMP
Two jumps in the air. Tap down to drop through platforms or taunt.
TAUNT
Show off between hits — some achievements track taunt count.
