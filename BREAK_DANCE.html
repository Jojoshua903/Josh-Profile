How to Play

🕺 You are Dean. Battles are rhythm duels.

➡️ Arrow notes scroll toward the golden zone on the left. Hit the matching arrow key (← ↑ ↓ →) the instant a note lands in the zone.

🎯 PERFECT timing deals big damage and builds your combo. Miss a real note and your rival scores on you.

✕ Fake notes (grey ✕) are traps — do NOT hit them.

🎵 Chain PERFECT hits to charge the BREAKBEAT meter. When it's full, press SPACE to unleash Dean's signature Breakbeat finisher — a huge burst of damage.

🏆 First flow bar to empty loses the round.

🎚️ Five difficulties from Chill to Nightmare — higher tiers mean faster notes, tighter windows, more fakes, harder punishment.

🔥 Side modes: Boss Gauntlet (all six on one bar), Survival, 60s Rush, and Free Battle.

🏠 Between story battles you hang out in Dean's HQ — walk around with WASD/arrows, poke at the boombox, trophies, fridge and more, then step into the ring to fight.
