mine deep · hunt monsters · get paid

A stout dwarf-for-hire in a mountain full of ore, wyrms, and odd little folk.

❓ How to Play

Move with WASD or arrows. Walking into dirt, stone, or ore mines it automatically; walking into an enemy attacks it.

Ground Pound (Space / click): a shockwave that shatters everything around you and damages nearby foes. It charges as you mine stone — watch the Pound meter.

Crossbow (F): a ranged weapon you can buy from Fionn, fired in the direction you're facing.

Talk / interact (E): sell ore & upgrade at Fionn's hut, take jobs from the cave gnomes, light crystal beacons, or chat with Dih the worm and Niffo the otter.

Bring Fionn one of every mineral (8 total) to win. Beware the Deep Wyrms below. Every new game carves a brand-new cave.
